Influencers

Rosy De Santo

Rosy De Santo is celebrated for her luxurious, timeless fashion, with an emphasis on Italian elegance and refined simplicity. Her style is sophisticated and often features classic silhouettes.

Key Traits:
  • Emphasis on timeless elegance.
  • Classic silhouettes and luxurious fabrics.
  • Focus on refined, high-end fashion.
Annemiek Kessel

Annemiek Kessel embraces avant-garde fashion, with experimental designs and a love for unexpected combinations. Her unique style sets trends and challenges fashion norms.

Key Traits:
  • Avant-garde, experimental approach.
  • Emphasis on originality and bold choices.
  • Fusion of art and fashion in her style.
Luka Sabbat

Luka Sabbat is known for his casual, streetwear-inspired looks with a high-fashion edge. His style is effortlessly cool, blending luxury with a laid-back vibe.

Key Traits:
  • Streetwear meets luxury fashion.
  • Effortless, casual styling.
  • Influence on youth culture and trends.
Elise Cook

Elise Cook represents a free-spirited bohemian style, often featuring flowy fabrics, earthy tones, and natural materials. Her look is relaxed and nature-inspired.

Key Traits:
  • Bohemian, nature-inspired aesthetic.
  • Flowy fabrics and earthy tones.
  • Emphasis on a free-spirited lifestyle.
Lunna Perez-Garreaud

Lunna Perez-Garreaud showcases a bold, colorful aesthetic, with influences from Latin American culture. She is known for her vibrant, statement-making outfits.

Key Traits:
  • Vibrant colors and bold patterns.
  • Celebration of Latin American culture.
  • Statement-making, expressive style.
Emma Smith

Emma Smith is a minimalist at heart, favoring neutral tones, clean lines, and sustainable fashion. She advocates for simplicity and mindful consumption.

Key Traits:
  • Minimalist aesthetic with neutral palettes.
  • Focus on sustainable fashion.
  • Advocacy for simple, mindful choices.
