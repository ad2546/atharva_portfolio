Visualization 1: Formula 1 Telemetry Link

Visualizing Telemetry data extracted from the F1 2024 season. The data includes various parameters such as speed, throttle, brake, and steering angle, providing insights into driver performance and car dynamics.

Visualization 2: Fins of the Floodplain

This visualization explores the floodplain regions and the frequency of floods over time. It focuses on hydrological patterns and highlights critical areas affected by recurring floods. The data helps in understanding environmental impacts and planning preventive measures.

Visualization 3: Opioids Use Among College Students

This report delves into opioid usage trends among college students. It identifies patterns of substance abuse, demographic factors, and potential reasons behind the prevalence of opioid use in academic settings. The insights are valuable for policymaking and awareness campaigns.

Visualization 4: Impact of the Opioid Crisis in America

This visualization highlights the devastating impact of the opioid crisis in America. It includes data on overdose rates, geographic hotspots, and the socio-economic costs associated with the crisis. The goal is to raise awareness and support actionable interventions.
